# Perioskoup Landing Page — Complete Design Brief for Manus AI

## What is Perioskoup?
Perioskoup is the first AI-driven dental companion that bridges the communication gap between clinician and patient. It transforms complex periodontal data into actionable, visual patient habits. Think of it as Duolingo for dental health — but powered by real clinical data from your dentist.

**Target audience:** Forward-thinking dental practices and periodontists who want to increase treatment acceptance and patient compliance through premium technology.

**Business model:** B2B SaaS — dentists pay monthly, patients use for free.

---

## Design Direction: "Titanium Pro" — Apple Maximalist

**Inspired by:** Apple iPhone Pro landing pages, Linear.app, Raycast.
**Feel:** Quiet luxury meets old money tech. Precision engineering meets high-end healthcare. NOT generic SaaS. NOT startup-y. Think: the Rolex of dental software.

### Color Palette
- **Primary/CTA:** #C0E57A — Lime Green (buttons, highlights, active states)
- **Background:** #0A171E — Deep Navy (global page background)
- **Surface/Cards:** #1D3449 — Slate Blue (cards, bento modules, inputs) — OPAQUE, no glassmorphism
- **Text:** #F5F9EA — Off-White (headings, primary body)
- **Muted:** #8C9C8C — Sage Grey (secondary text, borders, inactive icons)
- **Dark:** #050C10 — Deepest shadows, footer background

### Typography
- **Display Headings:** Dongle, Bold, 120px-180px range. Tight tracking (-0.04em). Used for graphic impact — almost as image elements. NEVER set font-weight on Dongle (it only ships as Bold).
- **Section Headings:** Gabarito, Bold, 48px-64px.
- **Body:** Gabarito, Regular, 18px (160% line height). High legibility.
- **Labels/UI:** Gabarito, Medium, 14px (All caps, wide tracking +0.05em).

### Style Rules
- **No glassmorphism.** Solid opaque #1D3449 surfaces only.
- **Corner Radius:** 32px for large containers, 16px for inner elements.
- **Shadows:** Deep, diffused ambient occlusion. No hard drop shadows.
- **Section Spacing:** 160px between sections.
- **Animations:** Scroll-triggered reveals, smooth transitions, hover states with subtle scale + glow.

## SECTION 2: COMPARISON — "A Tale of Two Clinics"

**Purpose:** Show the before/after pain point narrative.

### Layout
- Two-column split with a central vertical timeline/divider
- Left column: "The Standard" (problems) — muted, less prominent
- Right column: "With Perioskoup" (solutions) — bright, lime accents

### Content
**Section label:** THE NEW STANDARD
**Headline:** A Tale of Two Clinics
**Subtitle:** Discover how AI-driven insights transform the daily chaos of periodontal care into a streamlined, patient-centric workflow.

**Row 1:**
- ❌ **Monday Morning Chaos** — Patient forgets floss instructions given last week. Hygienist spends 15 minutes re-explaining basics, putting the schedule behind.
- ✅ **Monday Morning Clarity** — Patient receives AI nudge based on pocket depth on Sunday. Arrives prepped with questions about their progress trends.

**Row 2:**
- ❌ **Charting Fatigue** — Manual charting errors slow down intake. Assistant struggles to hear measurements over suction noise.
- ✅ **Voice-Activated Flow** — "3-2-3." Voice-activated charting auto-syncs to the visual dashboard instantly. Zero friction, zero errors.

**Row 3:**
- ❌ **Treatment Stalls** — Treatment acceptance stalls at 40%. Patients don't "feel" the urgency of 5mm pockets because they can't see the risk.
- ✅ **Visual Conversion** — 3D Heatmaps boost acceptance to 85%. Patients see the "red zones" on their own phone and ask: "How do we fix this?"

**Row 4:**
- ❌ **Clinician Burnout** — Exhaustion from repetitive explanations. The team feels like they are selling dentistry rather than treating health.
- ✅ **Automated Education** — Automated education handles the basics before the patient sits down. The team focuses on complex care and relationships.

**CTA:** [See the Dashboard →]

## KEY CONSTRAINTS
- **Pricing section MUST be blurred** with a "We're in Beta" overlay — do NOT show real prices
- **SaMD (Software as Medical Device) safe language** — avoid claiming to diagnose or treat; use "companion", "engagement", "tracking"
- **WCAG 2.1 AA accessible** — contrast ratios, keyboard nav, screen reader support
- **Mobile-first responsive** — desktop is the hero experience but mobile must be flawless
