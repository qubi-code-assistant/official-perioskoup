<idea>
**Design Movement:** Titanium Pro — Apple Maximalist Dark
**Core Principles:**
1. Precision over decoration — every element earns its place
2. Deep navy canvas with lime-green as the singular accent
3. Typographic hierarchy as visual architecture (Dongle display + Gabarito body)
4. Scroll-driven narrative — the page tells a story as you move through it

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: #0A171E (deep navy — authority, trust, clinical precision)
- Surface: #1D3449 (slate blue — opaque cards, no glassmorphism)
- Accent: #C0E57A (lime green — life, growth, health, CTA energy)
- Text: #F5F9EA (off-white — warm, readable, premium)
- Muted: #8C9C8C (sage grey — secondary, borders)
- Shadow: #050C10 (deepest dark — footer, depth)

**Layout Paradigm:** Asymmetric split layouts — hero is 40/60 left-text/right-phone. Sections alternate alignment. Bento grid for features. Timeline for workflow. Never centered-only.

**Signature Elements:**
1. Lime green left-border accent on blockquotes and key callouts
2. Circular grayscale founder photos with lime ring border
3. Animated counter numbers (30+) with scroll trigger

**Interaction Philosophy:** Scroll-triggered reveals using Framer Motion. Hover states with subtle scale(1.02) + lime glow. No jarring transitions — everything is fluid and deliberate.

**Animation:** IntersectionObserver-based fade-up-and-in for sections. Staggered children animations. Bouncing chevron scroll indicator. Typing indicator dots in AI chat mock.

**Typography System:**
- Display: Dongle Bold, 120-180px, tracking -0.04em (graphic impact)
- Section heads: Gabarito Bold, 48-64px
- Body: Gabarito Regular, 18px, 160% line-height
- Labels: Gabarito Medium, 14px, all-caps, tracking +0.05em

**CHOSEN APPROACH: Titanium Pro — Apple Maximalist Dark**

This is the design philosophy that perfectly matches the brief. Deep navy (#0A171E) as the global canvas, lime green (#C0E57A) as the singular life-force accent, Dongle for massive display typography, and Gabarito for all body text. Opaque slate-blue cards (#1D3449), no glassmorphism, 32px radius on large containers, scroll-triggered Framer Motion animations throughout.
